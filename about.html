About Me





Hey there! I'm LaWanda, and I'm super glad you popped in! Over the years, I've crafted my very own life blueprint, and let me tell you, it’s been a wild ride! I’ve run a non-profit organization called PurposeBound Parents, I was the principal owner of Refurbished Collectives—a cool vendor marketplace, and now I’m channeling my creativity as an aesthetics furniture designer for both folks and businesses! And guess what? As the proud owner of Designs In Color, I’m here to sprinkle some magic dust on your small business dreams!
I totally get it—the wild ride of being a small business owner! You’re juggling a million things and sometimes it feels like you’re on a deserted island. You’re pouring your heart into branding, wrangling social media, and now you’re like, “Wait, I need a website, too?!” Fear not, my friend! I’m ready to join your squad! I’ll whip up a fabulous website that won’t break the bank. Just tell me your budget, and I’ll work my magic. Plus, I’ll teach you how to keep it fresh and updated, so you won’t have to shell out extra cash to a developer for every little tweak. LET’S CHAT!
